Mac and Cheese Recipe

Rich and creamy mac and cheese perfect for the holidays and makes for a comforting family dinner!

Ingredients

  • Uncooked elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • All purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Cook macaroni according to the package directions. Drain the macaroni after.
  3. Melt butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Gradually add flour, whisking until well combined. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly until smooth.
  4. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick.
  5. Put macaroni in large casserole dish, and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.
  6. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown. Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle with a little paprika.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  8. Serve.
